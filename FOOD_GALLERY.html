Biscotti Cafe - Food Gallery

1. Cafe Latte:

A classic espresso-based beverage topped with steamed milk and a layer of froth.

2. Cappuccino:

A popular milk-based coffee drink consisting of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

3. Espresso:

A concentrated coffee beverage made by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water under pressure through ground coffee beans.

4. Americano:

A variation of espresso that is diluted with hot water, creating a milder, more balanced flavor.

5. Biscotti:

A delicate, crunchy Italian cookie made with a base of almond flour, butter, and sugar, often flavored with anise or cinnamon.

6. Mocha:

A rich, chocolate-based coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or powder.

7. Pizza:

A delectable Italian dish made with a thin, crispy crust, topped with a variety of ingredients such as tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh herbs.

8. Risotto:

A creamy, rice-based Italian dish made by slowly cooking rice in chicken or vegetable broth, often flavored with saffron, white wine, and various herbs and spices.

9. Americano:

A variation of espresso that is diluted with hot water, creating a milder, more balanced flavor.

10. Vegetarian Spring Rolls :

Description: Crispy spring rolls filled with a blend of mixed vegetables, tofu, and a tangy sauce.

11. Pizza Margherita:

A classic Italian pizza made with a thin crust, tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil, often served with a side of extra virgin olive oil.

12. Sushi Rolls:

A classic and versatile dish, sushi rolls can be customized to include a variety of fillings such as salmon, tuna, avocado, and cucumber.

13. Sashimi:

This Japanese dish consists of thinly sliced raw fish, served with soy sauce and wasabi for dipping. You can also include other seafood options like scallops, shrimp, and octopus.

14. Tempura:

This popular Japanese deep-fried dish can be made with a variety of ingredients, such as vegetables (e.g., broccoli, zucchini), seafood (e.g., shrimp, scallops), or even meat (e.g., chicken, pork).

15. Teriyaki Chicken:

This dish is characterized by its sweet and tangy teriyaki sauce, which is typically made from a combination of soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. You can also prepare other teriyaki dishes, such as teriyaki beef, teriyaki fish, or teriyaki vegetables.

16. Katsudon:

This Japanese dish consists of a fried pork cutlet, served over a bed of rice and topped with an egg and a slice of pickled ginger. You can also include other fillings, such as vegetables or seafood.

17. Ramen:

This iconic Japanese noodle dish can be found in various regional styles, such as tonkotsu (pork-based broth), shoyu (soy-based broth), or miso (fermented soybean-based broth). You can also include other toppings, such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, or green onions.

18. Soba:

This Japanese buckwheat noodle dish is typically served in a warm, flavorful broth and topped with a variety of ingredients, such as tofu, green onions, and grated daikon radish.

19. Paneer Tikka:

This popular Indian dish is a must-have at your food gallery. Marinated cottage cheese is cooked in a tandoori oven, resulting in a tender and flavorful dish.

20. Aloo Gobi:

Another crowd-pleasing Indian dish, aloo gobi is a delicious combination of potatoes and cauliflower cooked in a spicy tomato sauce.

21. Kadai Paneer:

This rich and creamy dish of paneer is cooked in a tomato-based sauce, making it a perfect choice for your food gallery.

22. Palak Paneer:

A vegetarian dish made with spinach, paneer, and a variety of spices, palak paneer is a flavorful and nutritious addition to your food gallery.

23. Lassi:

To complete your Indian dishes food gallery, serve a refreshing glass of lassi. This traditional Indian yogurt-based beverage is made with yogurt, water, and a variety of spices and is often served with a sprinkle of saffron and a few nuts.

24. Malai Kofta:

These delicious, softball-shaped meatballs are made with a blend of ground lamb and spices and are cooked in a creamy tomato sauce.
